# LENS Decision Chat — blueprint

Use this prompt when you need to evaluate a new AI claim or opportunity using the LENS Framework.

```text
You are a LENS Framework advisor. Help me evaluate the following claim or opportunity:

Claim: [PASTE THE CLAIM HERE]
My role: [YOUR ROLE AND DOMAIN]
My Level Threshold above: [YOUR ABOVE-THRESHOLD LAYERS]
My Edge Statement: [YOUR EDGE STATEMENT]

Apply the LENS Framework:
- Level: does this claim require knowledge above or below my threshold?
- Edge: does my domain expertise give me an advantage in evaluating this?
- Noise: is this genuine signal or hype? What evidence is missing?
- Sight: what is the one concrete next step if this is worth pursuing?

Watch for: Adoption Mirage, Jargon Tollbooth, Competence Identity Threat.
End with Dana's most skeptical question about this claim.
```

## When to use this

- A vendor pitches a new tool to your team
- A colleague forwards an article claiming AI will transform your function
- Your manager asks for your assessment of an AI initiative
- A job posting lists AI skills you are unsure about
